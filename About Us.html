ABOUT ISINGIRO SECONDARY SCHOOL

Knowledge Is Gold

School History

Isingiro Secondary School was established in 1979 to provide quality secondary education
to the students of Isingiro district and the surrounding areas.
The school started with a humble beginning, but over the years,
it has grown to become one of the leading secondary schools in the region.

Mission and Vision

Our mission is we strive for academic excellence,
and personal development so that each individual may achieve his/her potentials.

Our vision is to be a leading modern school producing moral upright,
academicallyexcellent,self reliant and responsible citizens.

Facilities

Isingiro Secondary School boasts a well-equipped campus with modern facilities to support
the learning and development of our students. Our facilities include:

  • Spacious and well-ventilated classrooms
  • Well-stocked library with a variety of books and resources
  • Science laboratories equipped with the latest equipment
  • Computer lab with access to the internet and educational software
  • Sports facilities, including a playing field, basketball court, and gymnasium
