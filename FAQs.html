Frequently Asked Questions

What are the admission requirements?

To be admitted to Isingiro Secondary School, students must meet the following requirements:

  • Successfully complete primary school education
  • Submit a completed application form
  • Provide copies of academic transcripts and certificates
  • Participate in an admission interview (if required)

What academic programs are offered?

Isingiro Secondary School offers a range of academic programs, including:

  • General Secondary Education (O-Level)
  • Advanced Secondary Education (A-Level)

What are the school's extra-curricular activities?

Isingiro Secondary School provides a variety of extra-curricular activities for students, such as:

  • Sports teams (football, basketball, athletics)
  • Drama and music clubs
  • ICT and Debate clubs plus many other clubs
  • Environmental and community service clubs

What are the school's facilities?

Isingiro Secondary School has a well-equipped campus with the following facilities:

  • Modern classrooms with multimedia technology
  • Well-stocked library and computer labs
  • Science laboratories for practical experiments
  • Sports fields, courts, and a gymnasium
  • Cafeteria and student dormitories
